Virtual Reality in Technology

VR technology goes beyond the use of computer graphics and HDMs. New technologies such as haptic clothing, eye-tracking technologies, and other sensory stimulation technologies are becoming popular and applicable in fields such as entertainment, medicine, education, and many other realms.

Virtual Reality in Health

In the field of Health and Health Care, VR is used for Physical Rehabilitation and Therapy, Cognitive Rehabilitation, Fears and Phobias, Pain management, and for Surgical and Medical Training. These applications of VR are improving healthcare at large through better medical training, and enhanced patient education and engagement in treatment.
